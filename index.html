How FitAI measures from camera views

Capture, measure, and style in three simple steps for stores, apps, or fitting kiosks.

01

Capture

Ask the person to stand straight in front of the camera. Front, side, and optional back views can be captured or uploaded from a phone.

02

Measure

Your AI model detects landmarks, corrects for camera distance, and estimates measurements such as height, chest, waist, hips, shoulder width, and inseam.

03

Recommend

FitAI maps measurements to kids and adult size ranges and suggests outfits for work, weekends, school, and special events.
